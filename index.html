Manufacturing & Sustainability

State-of-the-Art Infrastructure

Our facility in Urla Industrial Area, Raipur, combines modern refinery technology with traditional sourcing wisdom. With a 1,200 MT/month refinery (enhanced from 750 MT in FY25) and a 600 MT/month acetone fractionation plant (scaling to 1,000 MT), we process exotic oils — Sal, Shea, Mango, and Kokum — to the highest international standards.

We source raw materials sustainably from the forests of Chhattisgarh, Odisha, Madhya Pradesh, and Jharkhand, supporting local communities and preserving ecological balance.
